## 2024-05-24 - Canvas Critical State Announcements
**Learning:** In WebGL/Canvas interfaces (like the simulator), visually obvious critical states (like crashing or stalling) can easily be missed by assistive technologies if they are strictly visual overlays or drawn onto the canvas. Placing standard ARIA attributes (`role="alert"`, `aria-live="assertive"`, `role="status"`, `aria-atomic="true"`) on standard DOM overlays positioned above the canvas ensures these critical updates are aggressively and clearly announced by screen readers without requiring complex focus management.
**Action:** When adding new transient game states (e.g. paused, warnings, achievements) that appear visually on top of the canvas, ensure their corresponding container elements have appropriate ARIA live regions and roles so they are accessible to non-visual users.
